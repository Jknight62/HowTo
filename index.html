How To Create a Spray Paint Painting

Making a spray paint paint is a lot of fun and anyone can do it!


To begin, you will need certain supplies.

Supplies

  • Spray Paint (multiple colors)
  • Canvas (18x20)
  • Large bowls
  • Smaller bowls
  • Any other item to make design
  • Table to paint on
  • News Paper to pertect table

Creating the Painting

The first thing is to place the news paper on the table, then place the canvas on the table. Next select a paint color and spary an area on the canvas, just larger than your largest bowl.
