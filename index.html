How To Create a Spray Paint Painting

Making a spray paint paint is a lot of fun and anyone can do it!


To begin, you will need certain supplies.

Supplies

  • Spray Paint (multiple colors)
  • Canvas (18x20)
  • Large bowls
  • Smaller bowls
  • Old magazine
  • Any other item to make design
  • Table to paint on
  • Newspaper to pertect table

Creating the Painting

First Step

The first thing is to place the newspaper on the table, then place the canvas on the table.

Next select a paint color and spary an area on the canvas, just larger than your largest bowl.

Second Step

Take page of the magazie, crumple it up and dab the wet paint. This will create a textured look.

Do this for any other size bowls you have.

Third Step

Place your bowls over the spots you have painted.

Select a new color (black) and spray around your bowls, this will create a circle of the textured paint.

Remove the bowls, you will see that you now have Planets on your canvas

Finishing the Painting

Now that you have planets, and black as space, you can add more details.

Flick some white drops to create stars.

Add some more colors to your planets

Conclusion

It is a lot of fun creating painting with spray paint. If you imcorporate more stencils into your artwork it can turn into a wonderful picture. Anyone one with the ability to spray paint, is able to create a beautiful painting is just a little while.
